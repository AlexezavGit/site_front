# FEEL Again Program \- Official Positioning Statements

## **Формулювання позиціонування / Positioning Formulations**

# 🎯 Core Positioning

### Primary Definition

FEEL Again digital-ready Program in MHPSS.  
Программа призвана быть Service Infrastructure for Mental Health Coordination in Ukraine:

* supporting the beneficiary and provider through the digital communication protocol в процессе получения услуг;  
* mobilise local communities, corporate philanthropy, and local funds, with responsibility localization   
* Make risks go down, so resources and time are growing.  
* Make trustful digital interactions with global actors as invisible tech linked to best practice, standards, policies, and procedures, and build the coordination and simplify it  through acceptable digitalization   
* de-personified registry integration for maximum personal security, with open data set making   
* tracking interaction between every user and any users, didn’t depend on their occupation in organization or private practice, every session comply  
* KYC of users в момент ответственного или ценностного действия   
* AR/VR/AI tech. UA military grade, USA and Israel best practice on board, 

Alternative Formulations:

1. Fintech Bridge \- Фінансовий міст між донорами та локальними провайдерами  
2. Financial Rails \- Фінансові рейки для множини компаній кластеру \- локомотивів ментального здоров'я в Україні  
3. Humanitarian Financial leveridge  \- Інфраструктура гуманітарних платежів

4\. Transparency Layer \- Шар прозорості для MHPSS екосистеми  
5\. Coordination Protocol \- Протокол координації між глобальними та локальними акторами

Functional Descriptions

\*\*Technical Infrastructure:\*\*  
\- Technical infrastructure for coordinating between global actors and MHPSS cluster organizations (300+), service delivery to beneficiaries  
\- Network expertise and best practice training for therapists and psychologists  
\- Starting with GEHA (Israel) Training: clinical interventions in PTSD, ASD, EMDR Israeli clinical protocol \+ VR best practice by ICT

\*\*Data Aggregation Platform:\*\*  
\- Supporting mhGAP reporting requirements and creating datasets for digital coordination  
\- FAP starts to collect regression research for needs and service delivery 2014-2024  
\- Front-end and backend for each organization with a public dashboard

\*\*Compliance Partnership:\*\*  
\- Compliance partner for global actors and local organizations  
\- Leverage point of multi-sourcing co-financing, from community/gromada level to global actors  
\- Grand Bargain localization facilitator

🌍 Market Access Vision

\*\*Capacity Expansion:\*\*  
\- Access to market capacity of \*\*10,000-12,000 mental health professionals\*\*  
\- Bringing psychologists out of the statistical shadow economy  
\- Creating new segments within the cluster:  
  \- VR therapy specialists  
  \- Peer support counselors  
  \- Community mental health workers  
  \- Digital mental health facilitators

\*\*Evidence from Global Practice:\*\*  
\- Yemen humanitarian blockchain case: 1.4 million beneficiaries served through blockchain cash transfers  
\- Jordan refugee camps: Digital identity and payment systems  
\- Kenya: M-Pesa integration for humanitarian cash transfers

Service Components

1\. \*\*Session Verification System:\*\*  
   \- Blockchain-verified session tracking with embedded clinical protocol requirements  
   \- Preventing deviation from established standards  
   \- Requesting WHO methodological and technical support  
   \- Initiating legislation case for adaptation in Ukraine by the MOH and the WHO

2\. \*\*Evidence Base Expansion:\*\*  
   \- Continuous expansion of evidence-based methods  
   \- Negotiations with the University of Groningen  
   \- Under the management of Taras Shevchenko University  
   \- Partners: GEHA, ICT, potentially Groningen and Oxford

3\. \*\*Center of Expertise:\*\*  
   \- Selection of protocols and methodologies from numerous projects  
   \- Best practice identification and dissemination  
   \- Training and certification programs
